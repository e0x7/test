# Learn Quickly Cheat Sheet

You need 2 things to learn something. 

	1. Understanding what something is or what something means.
	2. Understand how it works.

If you can understand these two points. Then you will learn every fast and consider to be a expert.

Remember, learning bits of anything will lead to mastery when you use it.

1. What is {Keyword}? || What does {keyword/sentence} mean?
2. How does {keyword/sentence} work?

If you can answer these two questions and implemente it then you know it. 

This is how you know when you know.
